About Us?

We're more than just a cup of coffee


We're here to brew up a community that's as rich and flavorful as our coffee. Rabbit Hole is a community hub where caffeine flows, vinyl spins, and good vibes fill the air. So come grab a drink, lose yourself in the music, and become part of something special.

Where Coffee Meets Culture


We're passionate about creating a space that's as warm and inviting as your favorite record. Whether you're a seasoned audiophile or a curious newcomer, browse our curated selection of vinyl. We've got something for everyone, from classic staples to hidden gems waiting to be discovered.

Melodies & More:
A Stage for Self-Expression


Music is the lifeblood of our community. Our intimate venue plays host to a diverse range of local and touring artists. We believe in fostering a platform for creativity and self-expression. So come hear some fresh sounds, discover your new favorite band, or even take the stage yourself.

A Safe Space for All


We believe that everyone deserves a place to feel welcome and respected.
Here at RAbbit Hole, you'll find a judgment-free zone where all walks of life can connect, relax, and be themselves.
